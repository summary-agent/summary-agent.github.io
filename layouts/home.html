✈ Out of office

Back from PTO? Read one digest, not 200 notifications.

Summary Agent syncs time off from tools like BambooHR and your calendar, so it knows when you're out: your team sees who's away and who's covering, and a catch-up digest covering exactly the days you missed is waiting when you're back. A week of merges, releases, and escalations in one read — minutes, not your whole first morning.

What you get

№ 1

One digest, every day

Connect GitHub and Jira, pick a time, done. Every day Summary Agent gathers PRs, issues, releases, and tickets — and AI writes the update a teammate would: what shipped, what's stuck, what needs you.

№ 2

Connectors, not copy-paste

Plug in the tools your team already lives in: GitHub and GitLab, Jira, Slack channels, Google email and calendar, and HR systems like BambooHR. Your tokens are encrypted in the OS keychain and never leave your machine in plaintext.

№ 3

Built for teams

A familiar, Slack-like desktop app. Digests land in a shared Updates channel everyone sees. Per-seat licensing with Google Workspace sign-in and an admin console for managing seats.
